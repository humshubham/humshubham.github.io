Rules

1. This game consists of 3 levels, amateur, intermediate and pro.
2. Each level has 6 riddles, answers to which are the names of characters that appear throught the MCU.
3. Riddles pertaining to a particular level will be available to solve.
4. All 6 riddles must be solved in order to go to the next level.
5. Answer all questions in lower case.
6. Put only one whitespace after each word.
7. Answer all riddles to defeat Thanos.
8. Good luck!
